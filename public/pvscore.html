The Platform

One window for complete solar maintenance.

PVscore isn't just another monitoring app — it's a complete digital O&M hub. Track string performance, analyse efficiency drops, and get corrected PR ratios to keep every inverter at peak. Any brand, one unified interface.

01

Real-Time Inverter Health

Voltage, current and temperature analysed instantly. Get alerts the moment any inverter drifts outside ideal parameters — before faults appear.

02

Soiling Ratio Calculation

Dual-sensor comparison detects dirt-related energy loss in real time and triggers smart cleaning schedules to protect yield.

03

AI-Based Loss Detection

AI continuously studies inverter, irradiation, and temperature data to quantify every energy loss and name its cause.

04

Automatic Ticketing

Issues auto-generate a ticket, assign the right technician, and track response end-to-end — full O&M traceability.

05

Multi-Plant Dashboard

All sites under one login — generation, location analytics and health across your entire portfolio, unified.

06

Inventory Management

Centralised spare parts tracking with low-stock alerts, fully integrated with maintenance records and work orders.
